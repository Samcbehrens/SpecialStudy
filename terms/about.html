This is Sam Behren's Digital Art Special Studies. This piece engages one audience member in a room with Sam. With the audience members permission they will grab all the information they are allowed from google, youtube, facebook, and instagram. From this information they will collect their own information and will try to weave a relationship between the audience member's virtual identities and Sam's virtual idenity. They will also engage with the member in real space while highlight their virtual realities.
